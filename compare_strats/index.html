Strategy Architecture & Rules

1. V2 Option A Momentum: Relies on a Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM) index regime classifier. In a Bullish regime, it picks stocks with positive Supertrend direction ranked by 20-day returns. In a Choppy regime, it selects stocks trading below their Bollinger Band upper limits, scoring them by reversion distance.

2. Softmax Allocation: Weights the scored stocks exponentially, concentrating funds in the strongest configurations while distributing minor parts to avoid single-stock risk.

3. Nifty EMA-50 Switch (Bulletproof): Daily safety breaker. If the Nifty 50 close price is below its 50-day EMA, it liquidates all holdings at market close to sit in 100% Cash.
